Work

The Horde

  'The Horde' is a third person shooter wave spawning game developed in Unreal Engine 4 using a mix of C++ and Unreal Engine Blueprints. Being a solo project, all desgin, programming, animations and FX were done by me, using free assets acquired through the Unreal Marketplace. The game was designed around providing a simplified 'Call of Duty: Zombies' experience where the player fights through increasingly difficult waves of enemies until death, featuring basic thrid person locomotion, a two weapon system with varying ranges and damage applied through line traces, player stats such as health and ammo, item pick-ups for both health and ammo, 3 different types of enemies with varying stats and weapons, AI for enemies implemented through UE4 behavior trees, a mini map, UI and game settings.

Download: sentientaigames.itch.io/the-horde

Source Code: github.com/seanmavila/TheHorde

Cyberjump

  'Cyberjump' is a cyberpunk themed 2D platformer, solely designed and developed using C# in Unity Engine by me. Loosley based on the popular indie game 'Jump King', I designed this game to be a slightly hair-pulling experience that is sure to excite, and frustrate, even the most hardcore of gamers. Featuring locomotion and physics mechanics akin to the one and only 'Jump King' including: precision jumping, bouncing, and friction, or lack thereof, throughout three dystopian zones.

Download: sentientaigames.itch.io/cyberjump

Source Code: github.com/seanmavila/CyberJump
